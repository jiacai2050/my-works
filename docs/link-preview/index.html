Your Instant Window to the Web

Hover over any link to peek at what's behind it — images, videos, articles — without opening a new tab. Browse faster, decide smarter.

Chrome Firefox Edge

How It Works

1

Hover Over a Link

Move your cursor over any hyperlink on any webpage.

2

Instant Preview

A sleek popup appears showing the title, description, and thumbnail of the linked page.

3

Decide & Move On

Quickly assess relevance — click to open or move on. No unnecessary tabs.

Why Link Preview?

Save Time

Stop opening tabs just to check what's behind a link. Preview instantly without leaving your page.

Rich Previews

See titles, descriptions, and thumbnails — works with images, videos, articles, and more.

Works Everywhere

Built with WebExtension APIs. Available for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.

Lightweight & Non-intrusive

Minimal footprint. Preview only appears on hover — never gets in your way.
